The first 2 variables are identifiers for the subject participating in the experiment and activity performed by the subject.

subject - subject ID. 1, 2, 3, 4,...30
activity - activity label "laying", "standing", "sitting", "walking", "walking downstairs", "walking upstairs"

The next 79 variables are means of a set variables derived from time domain/frequency domain signals. The signals come from the accelerometer and gyroscope of a Samsung smartphone.
In general-
1. X/Y/Z direction is the record of the signal in that axis
2. Linear Acceleration is measures in meters/second(squared)
3. Angular Velocity is measured in degrees/second
